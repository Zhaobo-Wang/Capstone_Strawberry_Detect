Objective

We aim to enhance efficiency, precision, and safety in the process of spraying pesticides by developing the strawberry pesticide spraying robot. The robot will offer functions including mobile control via mobile platform, autonomous operation to navigate through the strawberry field automatically, obstacle avoidance, adjustable nozzle to suit plants' height and computer vision to identify specific areas of the strawberry plants that require pesticide treatment.
